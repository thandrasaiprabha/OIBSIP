Lee Byung-chul

1910 - 1987

Eureka Moment - The History Of Samsung.

Biography

Lee Byung-chul was a South Korean businessman and the founder of Samsung Group, one of the largest and most successful conglomerates in the world. Born on February 12, 1910, Lee started Samsung as a trading company in 1938, and under his leadership, it diversified into various industries, including electronics, shipbuilding, and insurance. He played a crucial role in the industrialization and economic development of South Korea. Lee Byung-chul passed away on November 19, 1987, but his legacy continues through Samsung's global impact.
